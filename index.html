Lincoln Community Playhouse




The playhouse has been active in Lincoln since 1946 and offers many different projects for the community to take part in including several shows each season, theatre arts academy camps during the summer, senior shows, and the Penguin Project mentioned below.


Penguin Project


From their website, the Penguin Project is focused on helping children with special needs “By providing access to community theater, The Penguin Project demonstrates that the special challenges of a disability should not handicap a child’s ability to participate in life’s experiences.” Larisa became involved in the project the first year the Lincoln Community Playhouse took part and has seen it grow exponentially each season.


University of Nebraska


Larisa attended the University of Nebraska- Lincoln campus. She graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and since continued her education at Doane University to become a school counselor.
